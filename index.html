Hungry People In Chicago


Oh….. there are hungry people in Chicago. Oh….. those miserable people. Oh….. we should help them.

1 in 4 children in Cook County are at risk of hunger. Latinos in Chicago have the greatest likelihood of hunger.

Come alone and support your community by volunteering. You can do things like donate food, repack food, drive the Fresh Truck, work at Kids Days, etc. all at the Greater Chicago Food Depository

Those with low incomes can get a government white card. Whether or not you can get a LINK card depends on your salary and the number of people in the family.
